Classes

Boxing

Sud'n Impact can train you to do what has been around since the first Olympics. This program will work your endurance, movement, form, and power. The class is suited to work with any age. We have both beginner and advanced classes for people of different skill levels.

Kickboxing

Sud'n Impact can train you to use a martial art that has been created in Asia but Americanized in the 1970s. It uses different styles of boxing, tae kwon do, and many others. The class is suited to work with any age. We have both beginner and advanced classes for people of different skill levels.

Tae Kwon Do

Sud'n Impact can train you in the widely known Korean martial art style. If you are looking for something that your kid can do this is perfect for them. As it is best learned at a young age. The students are placed in rankings based on belt color.

Aerial

Sud'n Impact can train you to become an aerial dancer. This is a form of modern dance that is mostly performed in the air. This is includes but is not limited to hanging hoops and poles. Every student is worked with individually to help them grow as a dancer.
